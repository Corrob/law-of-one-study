# Suno Meditation Audio Prompts

Prompts for generating guided meditation audio using [Suno AI](https://suno.com).

Each meditation is based on specific exercises from the Ra Material (Law of One).
Generated audio files should be placed in `public/meditations/`.

## Audio File Naming Convention

Each meditation has a localized audio variant per language:

| Meditation | EN | ES | DE | FR |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Finding Love | `finding-love-in-the-moment.mp3` | `es/finding-love-in-the-moment.mp3` | `de/finding-love-in-the-moment.mp3` | `fr/finding-love-in-the-moment.mp3` |
| Seeing the Creator | `seeing-the-creator.mp3` | `es/seeing-the-creator.mp3` | `de/seeing-the-creator.mp3` | `fr/seeing-the-creator.mp3` |
| Balancing the Self | `balancing-the-self.mp3` | `es/balancing-the-self.mp3` | `de/balancing-the-self.mp3` | `fr/balancing-the-self.mp3` |

Use the same **Style** prompt for each language — only the **Lyrics** change.
Add the appropriate language instruction to the Suno style prompt, e.g. "sung/spoken in Spanish".

## 1. Finding Love in the Moment

**Filename:** `finding-love-in-the-moment.mp3`
**Target Duration:** ~10 minutes
**Source:** Ra, Session 10.14 — Exercise One

### Style

Ambient meditation guide, soft ethereal female voice, gentle crystal singing bowls, slow deep drone pad, 432 Hz tuning, spacious reverb, minimal arrangement, peaceful and warm

### Lyrics (English)

[Intro — 30 seconds of ambient tone]

Close your eyes, dear seeker.
Allow your breath to slow.
Let the world around you soften.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

Ra teaches that the moment contains love.
This is the lesson of our dance here.
The first exercise is the foundation of all that follows.

[Pause — 8 seconds]

Bring your awareness now to this moment.
Not the moment that has passed,
not the moment yet to come,
but this one. Right here.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

In this moment, there is something present.
Beneath the thoughts, beneath the noise,
there is a vibration, a feeling, a presence.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

This presence is love.
It was placed here by the Creator
for you to discover.

[Pause — 8 seconds]

You need not create love.
You need not earn love.
You need only see what is already here.

[Long pause — 20 seconds]

Let your awareness expand gently.
Feel the love in the breath entering your body.
Feel the love in the silence between sounds.
Feel the love in the space around you.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

If your mind wanders, that is well.
Simply return, gently,
to finding the love in this moment.

[Long contemplative pause — 60 seconds of ambient tone]

Ra says: upon this choosing
rests the remainder of the life experience of an entity.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

Each moment you choose to see love,
you strengthen this seeing.
Each moment is an opportunity, fresh and new.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

Rest now in this awareness.
There is nowhere to go.
Nothing to achieve.
Only love to notice.

[Extended silence — 120 seconds with very gentle ambient background]

When you are ready,
carry this seeing with you.
The moment always contains love.
You need only remember to look.

[Gentle fade — 30 seconds of ambient tone fading out]

## 2. Seeing the Creator

**Filename:** `seeing-the-creator.mp3`
**Target Duration:** ~15 minutes
**Source:** Ra, Sessions 10.14, 42.7, 44.10 — Exercises Two through Four

### Style

Ambient meditation guide, gentle androgynous voice, Tibetan singing bowls, soft warm synthesizer pads, subtle heartbeat rhythm at 60 BPM, 432 Hz tuning, vast reverberant space, slowly evolving

### Lyrics (English)

[Intro — 30 seconds of singing bowl]

Welcome, seeker.
This meditation follows three exercises
given by those of Ra.
Each builds upon the last,
expanding your vision of the One Infinite Creator.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

Let your body settle.
Let your breath become easy and natural.
Release what you are holding.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

EXERCISE TWO — Seeing the Creator in Another Self

[Pause — 8 seconds]

Bring to your mind's eye the image of someone you know.
Someone you see often.
Let their face appear clearly before you.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

Now look deeper.
Beyond the personality, beyond the role they play in your life.
Behind their eyes, within their being,
see the One Infinite Creator looking back at you.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

This being before you is the Creator,
experiencing itself,
learning, growing, seeking,
just as you are.

[Pause — 20 seconds]

Hold this seeing. Let it become natural.
The Creator in them recognizes the Creator in you.

[Long pause — 40 seconds with ambient background]

EXERCISE THREE — Seeing the Creator in the Self

[Pause — 8 seconds]

Now turn this gaze inward.
Imagine standing before a mirror.
See your own reflection.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

Look into your own eyes with compassion.
Look past the story you tell about yourself,
past the judgments, past the doubts.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

You are the Creator.
Not metaphorically. Not poetically.
You are the One Infinite Creator,
experiencing this life through these eyes.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

Accept this. All of it.
The light and the shadow.
The strength and the vulnerability.
All of it is the Creator knowing itself.

[Long pause — 40 seconds]

EXERCISE FOUR — Seeing the Creator in All of Creation

[Pause — 8 seconds]

Now let your awareness expand outward.
Beyond yourself. Beyond any single being.
To the whole of creation around you.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

The room you sit in, the earth beneath you,
the sky above, the stars beyond.
Every atom, every photon, every breath of wind.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

All of this is the Creator.
There is nothing that is not the Creator.
The universe is a single being, exploring itself in infinite ways.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

Rest in this expanded seeing.
There is no separation.
There has never been separation.
Only the One, appearing as many.

[Extended contemplative silence — 90 seconds of evolving ambient]

Gently, when you are ready,
bring this awareness with you.
The Creator is in every face, in every mirror,
in every leaf and stone and star.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

You need only remember to see.

[Gentle fade — 30 seconds of singing bowl fading]

## 3. Balancing the Self

**Filename:** `balancing-the-self.mp3`
**Target Duration:** ~12 minutes
**Source:** Ra, Sessions 5.2, 42.11 — The Balancing Exercises

### Style

Ambient meditation guide, calm grounded male voice, deep drone in C, subtle wind chimes, very soft piano notes, binaural undertone, 432 Hz tuning, grounding and centering energy, warm and non-judgmental tone

### Lyrics (English)

[Intro — 30 seconds of deep grounding drone]

Be at ease, seeker.
This meditation follows Ra's teaching
on the balancing of the self.
It is a practice of acceptance, not correction.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

Allow your body to relax fully.
Feel yourself supported.
You are safe here. You are held.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

Now gently review your day.
Without judgment, without story,
simply let the moments of your day
pass through your awareness.

[Long pause — 30 seconds]

As you review, notice:
Was there a moment that carried a charge?
An emotion that was strong?
Perhaps frustration. Perhaps joy.
Perhaps sadness or pride.
Any feeling that held weight.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

Choose one such feeling now.
Let it arise within you.
Do not push it away. Do not cling to it.
Simply let it be present.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

Experience this feeling fully.
Let it fill you.
There is no wrong feeling.
Every emotion is the Creator exploring itself.

[Long pause — 30 seconds]

Now, gently, without force,
invite the opposite feeling.
If you held frustration, invite patience.
If you held sadness, invite joy.
If you held pride, invite humility.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

Experience this opposite feeling.
Let it too be fully present.
It is equally valid. Equally real.
Equally the Creator.

[Long pause — 30 seconds]

Now allow both to exist together.
Do not choose between them.
In the space between the two,
there is a stillness.
A point of balance.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

This is acceptance.
This is the technique Ra describes:
not the suppression of feeling,
but the full experiencing of each charge
and its opposite,
allowing balance to arise naturally.

[Extended silence — 60 seconds of drone and subtle chimes]

Ra teaches that this daily practice
is the cornerstone of spiritual evolution.
Not perfection. Not transcendence.
But acceptance of the self, in all its facets.

[Pause — 15 seconds]

You are already whole.
The balancing simply helps you remember.

[Pause — 10 seconds]

When you are ready,
carry this balance with you.
Return to it tonight, and tomorrow night,
and each night that follows.

[Gentle fade — 30 seconds of drone fading to silence]
