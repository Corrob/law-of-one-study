## The Cyclical Arc (preserve across all songs)

- **The "cannot name" thread:** Song 1 *"it has no name to call"* → Song 2 *"a name they cannot find"* / *"a light I cannot name"* → Song 3 seed *"a pull I cannot name"* → Song 6 reveal *"the name you could not find back then."*
- **The Song 3 ↔ 6 first-listen hooks:** Song 3's seed plants *"a voice beneath the dark,"* *"the hand that pulls,"* and *"some part of me I lost."* Song 6 pays each off near-verbatim — *"the voice you couldn't place was mine,"* *"the hand you couldn't see,"* *"I sent the pull,"* *"the spark you couldn't see,"* and *"the self I used to be."* The Song 3 bridge melody also returns underneath the Song 6 reveal — a sonic fingerprint so the connection lands on first listen, not only on a re-listen. **Treat the melodic reprises as production requirements, not options:** (1) the Song 3 seed melody under the Song 6 reveal, and (2) a brief echo of the Song 2 motif planted inside Song 3 — so the web of callbacks is dense enough that *something* connects sonically even for an inattentive first listen.
- **The love/wisdom thread (4 → 5 → 6 → 7):** Song 4 foreshadows it (*"love is only half the light; / the colder half is what I seek"*) → Song 5 is that cold half (*"or only half the sun?"*) → Song 6 unites them (*"the cold light and the flame, now one"*) → Song 7 reprises the union (*"the cold light learned to love at last"*). This makes Song 5's chill an *earned descent*, not a detour.
- **Melodic motif:** introduced fragile in Song 2, returns transformed/triumphant in Song 6 (handled via Suno prompts).
- **Octave loop:** Song 1 opens *"The universe breathes out…"*; Song 7 ends *"…and begins again…"* and flows into Song 1's opening on repeat. The seam between them *is* the eighth density — which Ra says is also the first density of the next octave (28.15) — so the loop is not a fade but a *chosen* new beginning: the totality, in bliss, electing to fall back into finitude and start the climb again.
- **Core arc lines (keep stable):** the Song 3 bridge (the seed) is verbatim; the Song 6 verse 2 + chorus (the reveal) is preserved except one polished line ("…used to be myself" → "…used to be").
